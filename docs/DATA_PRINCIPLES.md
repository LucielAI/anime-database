# DATA PRINCIPLES

## 1. No Fan Wiki Bloat
The archive exists to surface **structural mechanics**, counterplay depth, and the energy economy. Every new entity node, causality event, or relationship edge must justify its existence through renderer payoff and thesis clarity. Vague lore dumps will be rejected.

## 2. Thesis-Driven Generation
The generator does not apply a rigid global template to every universe.
The engine adapts based on what matters:
- Is the universe causality-driven? (Timeline rendering)
- Is it relationship-driven? (Node Graph)
- Is it economy/combat driven? (Counter Tree)

## 3. Strict Lore vs. Sys Voice Separation
- **Lore Mode**: Narratively grounded text.
- **Sys Mode**: A highly specific, algorithmic voice dependent on the universe itself (e.g., JJK uses "Negative Energy Economy", AoT uses "Deterministic Loop"). Sys mode must never bleach into generic tech jargon if there is a more precise structural metaphor available.
